# About the Portfolio & Delivery Technology Radar

### Introduction

The Portfolio & Delivery Technology Radar is a tool to track and share technology
choices across our teams. It helps us align on which technologies to adopt, trial,
assess, or hold.

### What is the Technology Radar?

The Technology Radar provides an overview of different technologies, tools, techniques,
platforms, and tips that we consider noteworthy. It focuses on items that have recently
gained significance or undergone changes.

### How it is organized

Items on the radar are organized into four quadrants:

- **Techniques** — Methods and practices for software development and delivery
- **Platforms** — Infrastructure and platforms for running software
- **Tools** — Software tools for development, testing, and operations
- **Tips** — Practical tips and best practices

Each item is placed in one of four rings:

- **Adopt** — Proven technologies we recommend for broad use
- **Trial** — Worth pursuing in appropriate projects
- **Assess** — Promising technologies to explore when needed
- **Hold** — Consider alternatives for new projects
