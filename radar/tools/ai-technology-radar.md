A community-maintained technology radar specifically for sharing opinionated
information about AI technologies used in the Portfolio and Delivery organization.
Radiates experiences, successes, and lessons learned with AI tools, platforms,
and techniques.

Built on the open-source Thoughtworks radar visualization, this tool uses a
GitOps approach where teams contribute YAML files describing their AI technology
experiences. Each "blip" represents an AI technology positioned in one of four
rings (adopt, trial, assess, hold) and categorized by quadrant (tools, platforms,
techniques, tips).

## Key benefits:

* Distributed ownership via CODEOWNERS for different radar areas
* Schema validation ensures data quality and required contact information
* GitLab CI/CD automatically generates and publishes the radar
* Living knowledge base of real AI technology experiences (both successes and failures)
* Helps teams make informed AI technology choices based on peer experiences
* Captures not just what AI tech we use, but why we chose it and what we learned

The radar serves as both a communication tool and historical record of our AI
technology journey, enabling teams to learn from each other's experiences with
AI tools and avoid repeating mistakes.
