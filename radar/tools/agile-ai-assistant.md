AI-driven chatbot leveraging the InstructLab model trained on internal agile
methodologies and Red Hat-specific practices. Provides on-demand agile coaching
to teams and individuals who do not have access to a dedicated Scrum Master.

Currently being tested as "Gem" with some performance issues. Offers tailored
advice specific to Red Hat's culture, tools, and agile frameworks.
